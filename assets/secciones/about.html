I'm Luciana. Graphic Designer from Tierra del Fuego, Antártida e Islas del Atlántico Sur. Actually based in Buenos Aires where i work as a Freelancer Web Developer and as a User interface & User experience Designer.

Bienvenidos! Welcome!

'm obsessed with making things and even more obsessed with making things better. I've been in the business of creating since I entered to bachillor where i learned electronics and robotics. After graduating as a Graphic Designer from the University of Buenos Aires, I've been actively involved in the web design community for the last 6 years. I've designed websites for small businesses. I also started a design Apps and love making Editorial Pieces. My specialty is full website strategy, design and development, making Mock ups in Illustrator or Figma and turning it into beautiful, semantic HTML & CSS. My interests, however, extend beyond the web and I love helping people with branding, print design and marketing strategy. I even love making illustrations. When I'm not designing, I'm probably hanging out with friends, or taking a walk with my dog.
